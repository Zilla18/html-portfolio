Zilla'a Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

    • Instructions

    1. Mix the milk with the yeas, sugar, eggs.
    2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
    3. Add the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
    4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
    5. Transfer the dough to a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
    6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large triangle.
    7. Melt the butter for filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
    8. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
    9. Cut the roll into 3cm section and place flat into a baking tray.
    10. Preheat the oven for 350F or 120C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
